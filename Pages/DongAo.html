Dong Ao/Mei Hua Hu

13/05/2025

My friend came Taiwan to visit so I went on a little road trip with him and his family, and they took me to Dong Ao, a lesser known place of Yilan County. The seaside was so healing and I also tried new seafood dishes...we also cycled around Mei Hua Hu, a flower-shaped lake!
