Streets of Tainan

05/04/2025

I spent a day aimlessly walking around the streets of Tainan - most of these alleyways and old shops had hundreds of years of history, yet felt so modern and stylish. It felt like it's own cosy community hidden from the traffic on the roads. I also learnt that 蝸牛巷 was pronounced 'gua' and not 'wo'.
