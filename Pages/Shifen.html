Shifen

09/03/2025

We went there along with the photography club from my exchange school on a photography trip! Shifen itself is a really nice town and beautiful shophouses and lights. It was however very polluted and suffering from overtourism...really makes me think about the tipping point of when tourism becomes so much more harmful to both the environment and living conditions. We also met a random merlion there (?)
