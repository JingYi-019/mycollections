Beitou Market

06/02/2025 - 10/02/2025

Bustling and noisy, but not crowded. I thought there would be more elderly but surprisingly there were a lot of children and families (probably because it was a weekend). The second floor had a lot of food options and the first floor was filled with negotiations and bargaining, which was reminiscient of the wet markets here in Singapore.
