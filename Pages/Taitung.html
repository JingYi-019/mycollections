Green Island, Taitung

17/06/2025 - 20/06/2025

Green Island was a lively small island with beautiful scenery, cool breezes despite the heat and a perfect place for a summer getaway. Taitung was a quiet, hot area on the east coast of Taiwan. We learned a lot about the Amis indigenous culture there, and enjoyed the natural coast looking out to the Pacific Ocean.
