Gunma

03/05/2025 - 05/05/2025

I went to Gunma in Japan for a short trip, and was absolutely blown away by the weather, nature and views. The city was peaceful despite it being the Golden Week, but the outskirts and mountains were brimming with activity. We went to Mt. Haruna on the first day, and visited the Shorinzan Daruma Temple as well as the Flower Observation Field.
