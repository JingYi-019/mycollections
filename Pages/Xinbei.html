Xinbei Spring Festival

22/03/2025

The Spring Festival was blessed with great weather, and a fantastic lineup of artists. For once, I was glad for my camera battery to run out because it meant I could fully enjoy the music and the moment. It was then that I realised I was taking pictures to record my exchange, not coming on exchange to take pictures, even if this realisation was something so simple. I also managed to take a photo with Soulfa, an indie band I've been following for quite some time (not shown here)!
