Akita, Morioka

03/07/2025 - 10/07/2025

The northern prefectures, Akita and Iwate prefecture, had breathtaking views and a rich culture. The Akita Nairiku Line was a train service that brought passengers through the lesser-travelled places of Akita Prefecture, immersing passengers in blissful nature that seemed to be isolated from the rest of the world. Morioka was simultaneously quiet and lively, with shrines boasting its rich cultural history and a variety of delicious foods.
