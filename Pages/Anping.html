Anping Walking Tour

04/04/2025

Anping is a small town located in Tainan, the previous capital of Taiwan. On a free walking tour, I learned much about Taiwan's colonial history and Tainan's history as the land of salt exports, fishery communities and more. I also made some friends with the tour guides, these two German guys on a long vacation and we talked a lot about our experiences in Taiwan - had a fruitful conversation that day!
