Geese and Ducks!

03/04/2025

At the Chimei Museum in Tainan, I learnt much about animal history and battle combat history. However, what really caught my eye were the huge flocks of geese and ducks on the museum grounds -- and so I named this collection simply geese and ducks. The museum restricted photography in a lot of places, anyway!
