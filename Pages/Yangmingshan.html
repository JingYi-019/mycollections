Although the weather forecast said it would be 22 degrees, the sun hitting the mountain made it feel like 28. However, this also meant the sun was hitting the flowers beautifully, hence the glow in the shots! The cherry blossoms didn't disappoint, but on hindsight I might have reduced the texture of photos a bit much. Nonetheless, it was a pretty scenery and a fun walk with friends in spring.
