Pop Music Cultural Centre

12/02/2025

A cultural revolution. The design of the exhibitions and the fact that I had a free audio guide that played 5 songs from each time period the exhibition was based on added to the experience so much. I was immediately transported into the beginning of Taiwanese music, how it got politicised, and slowly how it became a culture. I also spent a good 1 hour in the recording studio exhibition even though it was the size of my dorm room.
