Songshan Airport

30/05/2025

The Songshan Airport Viewing Gallery was filled with airplane enthusiasts, and children who seemed to have pilot dreams. The clear skies made the sunset phenomenal, and the aircrafts even more so, especially up close. I also took the risk to follow a stranger up to the roofotp of a market, but was worth it in the end with amazing views of the sky and landing planes.
